# Crop Insurance in Sri Lanka – Insurance & Risk Management Analysis

## Project Overview

This university group project examined crop insurance schemes in Sri Lanka and their role in managing agricultural risks. The study focused on risk coverage, claim procedures, premium determination, and the effectiveness of insurance in protecting farmers against financial losses caused by natural disasters and other uncertainties.

## Objectives

* Understand the role of crop insurance in agricultural risk management.
* Analyze existing crop insurance schemes in Sri Lanka.
* Examine premium calculation and claim settlement processes.
* Identify challenges and opportunities in the agricultural insurance sector.

## Areas of Analysis

* Agricultural Risk Management
* Crop Insurance Coverage
* Premium Determination
* Claims Management
* Farmer Protection Strategies
* Emerging Technologies in Insurance
